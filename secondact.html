Name of the play: Hedda Gabler

Author: Henrik Ibsen

BERTA.

[Laying down the letter.]

I think I had better put out the lamp. It's smoking.

MRS. ELVSTED.

Yes, put it out. It must soon be daylight now.

BERTA.

[Putting out the lamp.]

It is daylight already, ma'am.
